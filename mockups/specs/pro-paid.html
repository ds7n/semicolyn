Semicolyn — Pro / paid scope

Free for the entire product. Pro is a one-time purchase, cosmetic / vanity only — for users who want to support development. No subscription, no feature gates, no upsell prompts anywhere outside the dedicated screen. Enterprise explicitly deferred.

The qualification rule

A feature qualifies as Pro only if it is cosmetic, optional, or a thank-you. The moment "I need Pro to do X" is a real sentence, the feature is wrong for Pro.

1 — About & Help row, free vs Pro

The only Pro entry point in the entire app: a row at the top of About & Help. Label and version-row badge change based on Pro state. No prompts, no modals, no badges anywhere else.

3 — Out of scope (v1)

What we are not building and why:

  • Subscription tier of any kind. Not freemium, not Pro+, not "Ultimate." One Pro SKU.
  • Upsell prompts elsewhere in the app. No onboarding nag, no post-Nth-connection prompt, no preference-change interstitial. The About row is the only entry point.
  • "Pro" lock icons next to non-Pro features. There aren't any free features to lock — cosmetic items either are or aren't shown based on Pro state.
  • Promo codes, gifting, referral programs. Add only with clear reason later.
  • A second color palette. Pro unlocks themes when they exist; v1 ships one palette. Listed in the included as "(when available)" so buyers know what they're getting.
  • Donation-only tip jar. Supporter badge + alt icons keep this a "Pro" purchase, not a donation, which sets clearer expectations.
  • Enterprise features. Audit log · team-shared host configs · MDM-friendly config · centralized policy enforcement · SSO · concurrent-device licensing · premium support. Captured for v2 consideration; none designed.
