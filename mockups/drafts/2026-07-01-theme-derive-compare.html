authored vs derived

Left = the hand-authored chrome. Right = derived from the raw palette alone (16 ANSI + fg/bg + accentSlot) via the rules: surfaces = bg tint-steps (+2/+5/+11/+18 L), muted = mix(fg→bg,.42), accent = a slot, highlight = lighten(accent,+14,×.82). ΔL is the lightness gap. If the two chrome mocks read the same, derivation can replace hand-authoring.
