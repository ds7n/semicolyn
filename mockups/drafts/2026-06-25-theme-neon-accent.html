semicolyn — neon accent exploration

neo → neon. Same cool near-black surfaces and verdigris success as today; the accent is electrified to a saturated glowing gold and given a bloom. Two intensities below, against the current theme pinned on the left.

Deliberate restraint: the glow is on accent chrome only — the prompt ~, the cursor, the active-pane border, the armed keybar slot, and the bell. The terminal's command/output text stays calm and glow-free (it's read for hours, and SwiftTerm renders it). This is what keeps "neon" from tipping into an unreadable hacker cliché.
